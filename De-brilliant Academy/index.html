De-Brilliant House ACADEMY

De-Brilliant House Academy, founded by Mrs. Adeyinka Oluwafunmilayo, is an academy committed to unlocking the brilliance in every learner through technology. Mrs. Adeyinka holds BSC Computer Education, University of Nigeria Nnsuka, PGD in Computer Science, Caleb University, Imota, Lagos, MSC Computer Science in Olabisi Olabanjo University, Ago-Iwoye, Currently on PhD in Computer Science. She has been in the teaching profession for 2 decades. About a decade administrative experience as a Head of School in Lagos. Early Jolly Phonics and Montessori Expert. IGSCE and Coding Instructor. Director, De-Brilliant House Academy.
